Crystalline Caverns

Come join in on a fantastic subterranian hunt for riches, glory, and power!

Crystalline caverns is a new roguelite platformer where you will explore pocket dimensions and face off against ferocious monsters to obtain the legendary crystal cores. Unlock their power and see how far you can pierce into the darkness.

  • Boss Fights:
    • Track and kill the guardian of each labyrinth, or be tracked
    • Intelligent behavior makes showdowns unpredictable and fun
    • Put an end to their reign or drown in a sea of adds
  • Fast Paced Combat:
    • Tight controls offer a high degree of control
    • Harness powerful ranged magic and keep your foes at bay
    • Master lightning fast melee and parrying for the inevitable close encounter
  • Statistics:
    • Become powerful by advancing with the crystal cores
    • Focus on the mechanics you like and customize your character
    • Many builds are possible, offering varied playstyles
    • Bosses pass their randomized stats to their adds
  • Procedural Level Generation:
    • At least one million unique levels exist
    • Automated playtesting ensures that each map is playable
    • Use a random seed or supply your own
  • Unique Exploration:
    • Seamless toroidal maps have no unnatural borders
    • Scour the caverns for the monolithic crystals within
    • Proximity to a crystal confers strong temporary benefits
    • Destroying the crystals yields cores, offering a more permanent form of power
